About PlantPal

Making Growing Plants Easy Using AI

What is PlantPal?

PlantPal is an AI-powered mobile app designed specifically for beginner gardeners who want to grow flowers, vegetables, and fruits in small spaces. Whether you have a balcony, rooftop, or tiny backyard, PlantPal provides the tools and guidance you need to succeed.

Our mission is to make gardening accessible, enjoyable, and successful for everyone, regardless of experience level or available space. We combine cutting-edge AI technology with practical gardening knowledge to create a personalized experience that helps you grow healthy, thriving plants.

Who is PlantPal For?

Beginner Gardeners

New to gardening? PlantPal guides you through every step, from choosing the right plants for your space to understanding when to water, fertilize, and care for your garden.

Small Space Growers

Limited to a balcony, rooftop, or small backyard? PlantPal helps you maximize your space and choose plants that thrive in compact environments.

Visual Learners

Our AI-powered photo diagnosis (CropVision AI) lets you simply take a picture of your plant to get instant insights about its health and care needs.

Goal-Oriented Users

Love earning rewards? PlantPal's gamification system turns plant care into an engaging experience where you earn points for completing tasks and can redeem rewards.

Our Mission

At PlantPal, we believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience the joy of growing their own plants, regardless of their experience level or available space. Our mission is to make growing plants easy using AI technology that provides personalized guidance, instant problem-solving, and a supportive community.

Our Core Values: Accessibility, Simplicity, Innovation, and Community. We're committed to helping beginner gardeners succeed and enjoy the rewarding experience of growing their own food and flowers.
