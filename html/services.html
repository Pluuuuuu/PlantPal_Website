PlantPal Features

Discover all the tools and features that make PlantPal your perfect gardening companion.

AI Plant Diagnosis

Use CropVision AI to take a photo of your plant and get instant diagnosis of any issues. Our AI identifies problems like pests, diseases, nutrient deficiencies, and provides expert recommendations for treatment.

Smart Care Plans

Receive personalized watering and fertilizing schedules tailored to your specific plants and growing conditions. Plans adapt based on your location, season, and plant types.

Plant Journal

Keep detailed notes, photos, and care logs for each plant in your garden. Track growth milestones, document changes, and build a comprehensive history of your gardening journey.

Reminders & Notifications

Never miss a watering, fertilizing, or pruning task with intelligent reminders. Get notified at the right time to keep your plants healthy and thriving.

Gamification & Rewards

Complete plant care tasks to earn points and unlock rewards. Stay motivated with achievements, badges, and redeemable points through our partner marketplace.

Marketplace

Browse and shop for seeds, tools, and supplies from our partner vendors. Find everything you need to start and maintain your garden, all in one convenient place.

Community Feed

Connect with fellow gardeners, share your progress, ask questions, and learn from the community. Celebrate successes and get support when you need it.

Seed Suggestions

Get personalized seed recommendations based on your space, climate, and preferences. Discover new plants that are perfect for your growing conditions.
